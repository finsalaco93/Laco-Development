About Me

Frontend Developer!

As a Frontend developer, I am responsible for the visual and interactive elements of a website or application. I use programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the design, layout, and functionality of web pages. I work closely with UX/UI designers to ensure that your website is user-friendly and easy to navigate. I have strong problem-solving skills and am able to adapt to changing technologies and trends.
